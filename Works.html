Pet System

The pet system has 4 parts: A pet shop, an egg system, a pet inventory and a pet equip/follow. The pet shop has a GUI with all the available pets on sale categorized with its pet type. Hovering the mouse over the pet image shows its name, its buff /effects, and its price. The egg system is just like a shop but the price is the same for all the pets and it will give you a random pet. The pet inventory has a GUI containing images of the pets owned by the player categorized with its pet type. Hovering your mouse over the pet image shows the pet name, and pet buffs/effects. Lastly, the pets are equippable and the pets will follow the player wherever it goes.

Dice Game

The dice game requires 2 players, the 2 players will need to seat at the table. One player will be in the red team and the other player will be in the blue team. Once both players hit the ready button, the dice game starts. The 2 players will take turns rolling their dice. Whoever gets the highest roll wins the round. First to get 2 round wins will win the dice game and get a reward.

Car Shop System

The car shop system that was requested to me was very simple. The player steps on a part, the car shop GUI shows up. The player clicks which car to buy, and the car will spawn on the platform.

Disclaimer: I did NOT script the cars. They were free models provided by the client.

Intel System

The intel system also requires at least 2 players. When a player clicks the "Get Intel Target" GUI button, the game will give a camera tool to that player. The game will also choose a random player as an Intel Target. The player then needs to click the targeted player while holding the camera withing 50 studs away.

Delivery System

In the delivery system, when the player presses the Delivery GUI button, a package will spawn at a random location. The player then needs to retrieve the package at the location by pressing F while within 5 studs away from the package. Then the game will choose a random delivery location where player needs to bring the package. The player gets a reward for delivering the package.

Arrest System

The arrest system works similar to the delivery system. The player presses F to arrest the NPC. Then the player needs to bring the retricted NPC to a specific location to get a reward.

Bounty System

In this game system, the game will choose a random player every 10 minutes to be marked with a $10,000 bounty. The players in the game will have 10 minutes to kill the player with the bounty. If a player gets the bounty, that said player will receive the bounty reward, but if the target survives for 10 minutes without getting killed, no one gets the bounty.
